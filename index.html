Dil Ki Sachai

Main tumhare bina theek nahi lagta

Dooriyon ne sirf itna sikhaya hai ke tum meri aadat nahi, meri zarurat ho. Tumhare bina din lamba lagta hai, raat sookhi lagti hai, aur har jeet aadhi lagti hai.

Kanchan, sach kahun to main strong ban kar rehta hoon, lekin andar se roz toot sa jata hoon jab tumhari yaad achanak dil par girti hai. Long distance job ne mujhe tum se door zaroor kiya hai, lekin meri har soch, har dua, har plan ka centre tum hi ho.

Tum naraz ho, aur shayad us narazgi ka haq bhi tumhein hai. Lekin ek baat bilkul saaf hai: mera pyaar acting nahi hai, habit nahi hai, aur timepass to bilkul nahi. Main tumhein waqai apni zindagi ka sab se khoobsurat hissa maanta hoon.

Tumhari awaaz se sukoon aata hai. Tumhari yaad se dil bhar aata hai. Tumhari muskuraahat ka khayal bhi mere din ka rang badal deta hai. Main yeh website is liye nahi bana raha ke bas tum maan jao. Main yeh is liye bana raha hoon ke tum dekh sako ke tum mere liye kitni gehri ho.

Sirf tumhara — Suresh

Sorry Note

Meri jaan Kanchan, agar meri kisi baat, kisi tone, kisi kami, ya kisi distance ne tumhein dukh diya hai, to main dil se maafi chahta hoon. Main perfect nahi hoon, lekin tumhare liye beparwah bhi nahi hoon. Main seekh raha hoon, sudhar raha hoon, aur tumhein khone ka khayal bhi bardasht nahi kar sakta.

Narazgi ka matlab yeh nahi hota ke pyaar khatam ho gaya. Kabhi kabhi narazgi ka matlab hota hai ke dil ne zyada feel kiya. Aur tumhara dil mere liye feel karta hai, yahi baat mere liye sab se important hai.

Main bas itna chahta hoon: ek mauka aur de do ke main tumhein sirf words se nahi, consistency se feel karaun ke tum meri priority ho. Main door hoon, lekin tumse door hona kabhi choose nahi kiya.

Love Note

Kanchan, tum meri life ka woh hissa ho jise main kisi aur cheez se replace soch bhi nahi sakta. Tum meri aadat nahi, meri dua ka jawab ho. Tum se baat ho to sab theek lagta hai. Tum chup ho jao to duniya ki awaaz bhi bekaar lagti hai.

Main roz sochta hoon ke jis din tum mere paas hogi aur doori khatam hogi, us din main sirf ek baat kahunga: dekha, mera dil sahi tha — meri duniya tum hi thi.

Mera ghar koi jagah nahi. Mera ghar tum ho. Aur main har thakan, har tension, har tanhai ke baad tum hi mein lautna chahta hoon.

Last Message

Bas ek baat aur...
